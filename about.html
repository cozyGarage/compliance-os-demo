About ComplianceOS

Founded in Vienna, we're building the future of regulatory compliance.

Our mission is to democratize compliance — making it accessible, automated, and affordable for every EU enterprise. No company should face regulatory penalties because compliance was too complex or too costly.

Our Story

In 2023, a group of compliance professionals and AI engineers in Vienna recognized a growing problem: EU regulations were becoming increasingly complex, yet the tools available to manage them hadn't evolved beyond spreadsheets and manual processes.

With the introduction of CSRD, NIS2, and AIFMD revisions, companies faced an unprecedented regulatory burden. Non-compliance penalties of up to 10 million EUR or 5% of yearly revenue made the stakes higher than ever.

ComplianceOS was founded with a simple premise: AI can transform how organizations approach regulatory compliance. By converting complex regulatory rules into machine-readable formats and automating data matching, we've reduced compliance lead time by 80%.
